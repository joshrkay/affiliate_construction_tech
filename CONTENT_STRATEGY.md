# Content Strategy for bestconstructionapps.com

## Applying content-strategy Framework

---

## Step 1: Define Content Goals

### Primary Goal: **Generate Leads via Affiliate Revenue**

| Goal | Implementation |
|------|---------------|
| **Generate Awareness** | SEO content targeting contractor searches |
| **Build Trust** | Comprehensive reviews with real user data |
| **Drive Leads** | Affiliate links to software tools |
| **Enable Sales** | Comparison pages answering buyer questions |

---

## Step 2: Research Audience Content Needs

### Target Audience: Small-to-mid construction business owners
- HVAC contractors
- Plumbing contractors  
- Electrical contractors
- General contractors
- Specialty trades

### Top Questions from Audience (Research-Based):
1. "What is the best software for [my trade]?"
2. "[Tool A] vs [Tool B] - which is better?"
3. "How much does [software] cost?"
4. "Is [software] worth it for small business?"
5. "How do I choose construction software?"

### Competitor Gaps:
- Most competitors have shallow 300-word reviews
- Missing detailed pricing breakdowns
- No "alternatives to [Tool]" content
- Limited trade-specific content

---

## Step 3: Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: Tool Reviews (Depth)
- Individual software reviews
- Pros/cons analysis
- Real user feedback
- Trade-specific recommendations

### Pillar 2: Comparisons (Commercial Intent)
- Tool vs Tool comparisons
- Best-for lists
- Alternatives pages

### Pillar 3: How-To Guides (SEO)
- Software selection guides
- Implementation guides
- ROI calculators
- Industry trends

### Pillar 4: Trade-Specific Content
- HVAC software guides
- Plumbing software guides
- Electrical software guides
- General contractor tools

---

## Step 4: Content Formats & Channels

### Primary Format: **Blog Posts (SEO-focused)**
- Evergreen content
- 1,500+ words per article
- FAQ schema for AI search

### Secondary Format: **Tool Pages**
- Comprehensive tool database
- Affiliate-ready CTAs
- User reviews

### Distribution Channels:
| Channel | Priority | Status |
|---------|----------|--------|
| Google/SEO | #1 | Building |
| Reddit | #2 | Ready to execute |
| Facebook Groups | #3 | Ready to execute |
| LinkedIn | #4 | Ready to execute |
| YouTube | #5 | Strategy ready |

## Step 5: Content Calendar

### Weekly Publishing Schedule:
| Day | Content Type |
|-----|--------------|
| Monday | 1 new tool review |
| Tuesday | 1 comparison page |
| Wednesday | 1 best-for page |
| Thursday | 1 how-to guide |
| Friday | 1 trade-specific article |
| Saturday | Update existing content |
| Sunday | Plan next week |

### Monthly Goals:
- 4 new tool reviews
- 4 comparison pages
- 4 best-for pages
- 4 how-to guides
- 4 trade articles
- 20 existing page updates
