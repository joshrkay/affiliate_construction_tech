# YouTube Content Strategy for BUILTECH

## 🎯 Goal
Drive traffic to bestconstructionapps.com through video content featuring software comparisons, reviews, and trade-specific recommendations.

---

## 📺 Video Format Ideas

### 1. Comparison Videos (High Value - 8-12 min)
**Format:** Side-by-side comparison of two tools
- "Procore vs Buildertrend: Which is Better for Small Contractors?"
- "ServiceTitan vs Jobber: Full Comparison 2026"
- "Togal.AI vs STACK Takeoff: Which AI Estimating Tool Wins?"

**Structure:**
- Intro (30 sec) - Hook viewers with the problem
- Quick overview of each tool (2 min each)
- Head-to-head comparison (4 min) - Features, pricing, pros/cons
- Verdict (1 min) - Who should choose what
- CTA (30 sec) - Visit bestconstructionapps.com for full reviews

### 2. "Best for" Videos (Trade-specific - 10-15 min)
- "Best Construction Software for HVAC Contractors (2026)"
- "Best Estimating Software for Electrical Contractors"
- "Best Field Service Software for Plumbers"

**Structure:**
- Intro with pain points
- Top 5 tools with quick pros/cons
- Detailed breakdown of top 3
- Price range discussion
- CTA with link to site

### 3. Tool Deep Dives (5-8 min)
- "Complete Procore Review 2026"
- "Is Jobber Worth It? Honest Review"
- "ServiceTitan Demo & Walkthrough"

### 4. "How To" Guides
- "How to Choose Construction Software in 2026"
- "How to Switch from QuickBooks to Procore"
- "How to Estimate Electrical Jobs with AI"

---

## 🎬 Production Checklist

### Equipment
- [ ] Good lighting (ring light or key light)
- [ ] Clear audio (lapel mic or shotgun mic)
- [ ] Screen recording software (Loom, Camtasia, or OBS)
- [ ] Video editor (DaVinci Resolve - free, or Final Cut)

### Script Template
```
[HOOK - 30 sec]
"Are you an HVAC contractor tired of wasting time on manual scheduling? In this video, I'm comparing the two most popular HVAC software options..."

[TOOL 1 OVERVIEW - 2 min]
"Let's start with ServiceTitan. It's the enterprise choice with these key features..."

[TOOL 2 OVERVIEW - 2 min]
"Now let's look at Jobber. Here's what makes it different..."

[HEAD-TO-HEAD - 4 min]
"Here's how they compare on pricing, ease of use, and features..."

[VERDICT - 1 min]
"So which one should you choose? If you're a small team under 5 techs, Jobber is the better choice. If you need enterprise features and have the budget, go with ServiceTitan."

[CTA - 30 sec]
"For more detailed reviews and comparisons, visit bestconstructionapps.com - we've got full reviews of both tools plus 30+ more construction software options."
```

## 📈 Distribution Channels

### Primary: YouTube
- Upload with SEO-optimized titles
- Description with timestamps and links
- Tags: construction software, HVAC software, contractor tools
- End screens and cards linking to other videos

### Secondary: Shorts/Reels
- 60-second highlights
- "Procore vs Buildertrend in 60 Seconds"
- Quick tool tips
- Behind-the-scenes

### Tertiary: Embed on Site
- Embed YouTube videos on relevant comparison pages
- Increases time-on-site
- Improves SEO
