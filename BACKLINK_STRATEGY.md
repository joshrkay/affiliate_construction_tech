## 📋 Link Building Tactics

### Tier 1: High-Value Links
1. **Guest Posts** - Write expert articles for industry blogs
2. **Digital PR** - Newsworthy content, statistics, studies
3. **Resource Page Links** - Get listed on resource pages
4. **Broken Link Building** - Find broken links, offer replacement

### Tier 2: Medium-Value Links
5. **Directory Submissions** - Industry directories
6. **Social Profiles** - Link from social profiles
7. **Review Sites** - Get listed on software review sites
8. **Partnerships** - Partner with complementary businesses

### Tier 3: Ongoing
9. **Content Marketing** - Create linkable assets
10. **Q&A Sites** - Answer questions, link back
11. **Industry Forums** - Build reputation, links
12. **Press Releases** - For major updates
