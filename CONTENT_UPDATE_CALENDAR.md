# Content Update Calendar

## Monthly Update Schedule

### Week 1 (1st-7th)
- Review top 10 articles by traffic
- Update pricing information
- Add new tools to comparisons
- Refresh statistics

### Week 2 (8th-14th)
- Review articles #11-20
- Update rankings
- Add new FAQs based on user questions

### Week 3 (15th-21st)
- Review articles #21-30
- Update industry statistics
- Add new comparisons

### Week 4 (22nd-28th)
- Add new tool reviews
- Update best-for lists
- Refresh any outdated content

---

## Date Stamp Format

All articles use ISO format: YYYY-MM-DD

Example: lastUpdated: "2026-03-20"
