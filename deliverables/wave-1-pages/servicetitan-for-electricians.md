## Quick Verdict
ServiceTitan is a leading platform for electrical service businesses that need dispatch, call handling, technician performance visibility, and revenue process control in one system. ## Best For / Not Ideal For
**Best for:**
- Electrical service businesses with multiple technicians
- Teams focused on operational metrics and call-to-cash performance
- Companies scaling dispatch and field accountability

**Not ideal for:**
- Very small teams with simple scheduling/invoicing needs

- Price-sensitive operators seeking lightweight tooling only
