# 01 — Keyword/Trend Research Map (SEO + AI Search Intent)

Status: Initial draft (local)
Date: 2026-03-06

## Objective
Build a demand-validated keyword and topic map for a construction tech/tooling site, optimized for:
- Traditional SEO intent capture
- AI-answer engine citation potential (clear entities, definitions, comparisons, checklists)

## Core Intent Buckets
1. **Informational** — “what is…”, “how to…”, “best…”, “vs…”, “cost…”
2. **Commercial investigation** — “best [tool type] for [trade]”, “reviews”, “alternatives”
3. **Transactional** — “buy [tool]”, “pricing”, “discount”, “near me”
4. **Problem-solution** — “why does [tool/problem] happen”, troubleshooting terms

## AI Search Intent Layer
For each keyword cluster, include:
- Direct-answer paragraph (40–90 words)
- Comparison table schema (feature, use case, trade fit)
- FAQ block (5–8 Q/As)
- Source-ready structure (definitions, specs, safety constraints)

## Priority Topic Clusters (V1)
- Cordless drill / impact driver comparisons
- Laser levels for layout (indoor/outdoor)
- Circular saw blade selection by material
- Oscillating multi-tool use-cases by trade
- Jobsite vacuums and dust control compliance
- Rotary hammers and concrete anchoring workflows
- Measuring/layout tools for framing vs finish
- PPE and safety selection by task

## Validation Workflow
1. Pull seed terms by trade + tool category
2. Expand with query modifiers (best, vs, size, cost, troubleshooting)
3. Score by:
   - intent quality
   - relevance to monetization
   - SERP competition (manual)
   - AI answerability (high/medium/low)
4. Promote top opportunities into content briefs
