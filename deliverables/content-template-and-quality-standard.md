# Construction SaaS Content Template + Quality Standard

Date: 2026-03-07
Purpose: Enforce consistent, publish-ready quality while producing pages at scale.

## 1) Required Page Template (every software page)

### H1
`{Software} for {Trade}: Features, Pros, Cons, Pricing, and Alternatives`

### Section Order (mandatory)
1. **Quick Verdict (60–90 words)**
2. **Best For / Not Ideal For**
3. **Key Features for {Trade}**
4. **Pros (5–7 bullets)**
5. **Cons (3–5 bullets)**
6. **Pricing Notes (transparent + caveats)**
7. **Trade-Fit Score (1–10) + rationale**
8. **Top Alternatives (2–3)**
9. **FAQ (4–6 Q/A)**
10. **CTA** (trade shortlist / compare options)

## 2) Quality Gates (must pass all)

### Accuracy
- [ ] Claims are concrete and non-generic
- [ ] Pros/cons are specific to trade workflows
- [ ] No fabricated pricing/details

### SEO
- [ ] One primary keyword
- [ ] Semantic secondary terms included naturally
- [ ] Title/H1 aligned with intent
- [ ] Internal links to trade hub + comparisons

### Readability
- [ ] Skimmable with headings/bullets
- [ ] First 120 words answer search intent
- [ ] Plain language, no fluff

### Conversion
- [ ] Clear “best for” guidance
- [ ] Practical decision support (who should/shouldn’t choose)
- [ ] CTA aligned to user stage

### Technical
- [ ] Slug follows standard
- [ ] FAQ schema-ready section present
- [ ] No broken links/placeholders

## 3) Writing Rules
- Avoid hype language and vague adjectives.
- Every pro/con must map to a real use-case.
- If unknown, say “varies by plan/vendor quote” explicitly.
- Prioritize decision utility over word count.

## 4) Scoring Rubric (100 points)
- Accuracy: 30
- Trade relevance: 20
- SEO intent match: 20
- Readability: 15
- Conversion utility: 15

**Publish threshold:** 85+

## 5) Assignment Flow
1. Research owner drafts evidence notes
2. Content owner writes full page using template
3. QA owner scores with rubric
4. SEO owner final polish
5. Publish
