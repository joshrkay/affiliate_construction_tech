# Daily Operations Plan

## Core Directive: ALWAYS BE PRODUCTIVE

---

## Daily Schedule (Every Day)

### 🌅 Morning (First Thing)

#### 1. Check Analytics (5 min)
- Check Google Search Console
- Check page rankings
- Note traffic changes

#### 2. Content Research (15 min)
- Check for tool updates
- Check industry news
- Note competitor changes

#### 3. Content Creation (30-60 min)
- Add 1 new tool OR
- Expand 1 article OR
- Add new comparison

---

### ☀️ Mid-Day

#### 4. Social Distribution (15 min)
- Post to Reddit (answer 2-3 questions)
- Engage in Facebook groups
- LinkedIn post

#### 5. Community (10 min)
- Reply to comments
- Answer questions
- Build relationships

---

### 🌆 Evening

#### 6. Content Updates (15 min)
- Update 1 existing page
- Add new FAQ
- Refresh pricing

#### 7. Plan Tomorrow (5 min)
- Note what to create next
- Prioritize high-value content

---
