## How to Choose the Right Software

Not every tool is right for every contractor. Here's how to pick:

### 1. Start With Your Biggest Pain Point

Don't try to solve everything at once. What's costing you the most time or money right now?

- **Estimating too slow?** → Togal.AI, STACK, Esticom
- **Field-office communication breaking?** → ServiceTitan, JobTread, Procore
- **Losing track of job costs?** → JobTread, Knowify, Procore
- **Document chaos?** → Bluebeam Revu

### 2. Match Size to Scope

- **1-10 employees:** JobTread, Jobber, FieldEdge
- **10-50 employees:** JobTread, ServiceTitan, Knowify
- **50+ employees:** Procore, Autodesk Construction Cloud
