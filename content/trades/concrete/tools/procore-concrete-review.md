# Procore Review: Construction Management for Concrete Contractors

Procore is the gold standard for construction management and is widely used by concrete contractors for commercial and infrastructure projects.

## What Procore Does

Procore handles:
- Project management
- Scheduling
- Budget tracking
- Field productivity
- Quality control

## Key Features

- RFI management
- Submittals
- Daily logs
- Change orders
- Budget vs actual
- Photo documentation

## Pricing

$15-$50/user/month + implementation

## Pros

- Industry standard
- Comprehensive
- Scales with you
- Strong integrations

## Cons

- Expensive
- Complex
- Implementation time

## Verdict

Procore is the best choice for concrete contractors doing commercial or infrastructure work.
