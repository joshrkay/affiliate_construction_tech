## How to Choose

### Consider Your Size
- **Small (1-10 techs)**: Jobber, Housecall Pro, or FieldEdge offer the best value.
- **Mid (10-20 techs)**: ServiceTitan or Simpro for more advanced features.
- **Large (20+ techs)**: ServiceTitan for comprehensive capabilities.

### Budget
- **Entry-level**: $49-79/tech/month (Jobber, Housecall Pro, FieldEdge)
- **Mid-tier**: $99-150/tech/month (Simpro)
- **Enterprise**: $199+/tech/month (ServiceTitan)

### Key Features Needed
- **Scheduling & dispatch**: All platforms offer this.
- **Invoicing & payments**: All include basic functionality.
- **Accounting integration**: ServiceTitan, Jobber, Housecall Pro lead here.
- **Marketing/CRM**: ServiceTitan offers the most robust.
- **Job costing**: Simpro and ServiceTitan excel.
