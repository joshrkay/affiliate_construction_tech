# mHelpDesk Review: Budget-Friendly Field Service Software

mHelpDesk is an affordable field service management platform designed for small contractors and service businesses. With pricing starting at $39/user/month, it's one of the most budget-friendly options for small teams.

## What mHelpDesk Does

mHelpDesk handles:
- Scheduling
- Customer management
- Invoicing
- Work orders
- Automated reminders

**Who it's for:** Small contractors, solo service providers, budget-conscious businesses.

## Key Features

### Scheduling
- Calendar view
- Technician scheduling
- Drag-and-drop
- Availability

### Customer Management
- Contact database
- Service history
- Notes
- Documents

### Invoicing
- Create invoices
- Send online
- Accept payments
- Recurring

### Automated Reminders
- Appointment reminders
- Follow-ups
- Confirmation requests

## Pricing

| Plan | Price |
|------|-------|
| Basic | $39/user/mo |
| Professional | $59/user/mo |
| Enterprise | $89/user/mo |

Annual billing saves 20%.

## Pros

- **Affordable**
- **Easy to use**
- **Mobile app**
- **Quick setup**
- **Good for small teams**

## Cons

- Limited features
- Basic reporting
- Fewer integrations
- May outgrow quickly

## Verdict

mHelpDesk is a solid budget choice for very small contractors just starting to digitize.

**Best for:** Small teams, solo contractors, budget-conscious businesses.

**Skip if:** You need advanced features or expect to scale quickly.
