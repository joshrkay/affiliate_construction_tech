# ServiceTitan Review: The Gold Standard for Plumbing Contractors

ServiceTitan is the most comprehensive field service management platform available for plumbing contractors. With over 70 integrations, real-time job costing, and enterprise-grade features, it's the choice of many of the largest plumbing companies in North America.

## What ServiceTitan Does

ServiceTitan handles every aspect of running a plumbing service business:
- Scheduling and dispatch
- Customer relationship management
- Invoicing and payments
- Job costing and analytics
- Marketing
- Inventory management
- Commercial project management

**Who it's for:** Mid-to-large plumbing contractors, service businesses with 5+ technicians, companies seeking to scale.

## Key Features

### Advanced Scheduling
- Drag-and-drop dispatch board
- Technician availability and skills matching
- Auto-scheduling algorithms
- Route optimization
- Real-time GPS tracking

### Real-Time Job Costing
This is where ServiceTitan shines:
- Track labor costs vs. estimate in real-time
- See profit margins per job instantly
- Historical job cost analysis
- Predictive pricing insights
- Materials tracking

### 70+ Integrations
Plumbing-specific integrations include:
- QuickBooks Online & Desktop
- Sage 100 Contractor
- XOi (field documentation)
- ServiceChannel
- Jobber (some use both)
- Ferguson (pro supply)

### Marketing Suite
Built-in marketing tools:
- Automated review requests
- Email campaigns
- Neighborhood campaigns (door-to-door tracking)
- Call tracking
- Customer portal with online booking

### Commercial Capabilities
ServiceTitan handles commercial work better than most:
- Project-based tracking
- Change order management
- Retention tracking
- Multiple location support

## Pricing

ServiceTitan doesn't publish pricing. Expect to budget **$75-$150/user/month** for most plumbing contractors.

**What's typically included:**
- Core platform (scheduling, CRM, invoicing)
- Mobile app
- Basic integrations
- Standard support

**Add-ons cost extra:**
- Marketing suite
- Customer portal premium
- Advanced analytics
- Implementation and training (can be $5K-$15K)

- **Strong commercial support** - handles big jobs
- **Best integrations** - 70+ connections

- **Sales process** - no self-serve, must talk to sales

## Verdict

ServiceTitan is the gold standard for plumbing contractors who need enterprise-grade features. It's expensive and complex, but if you're serious about scaling and need comprehensive field service management, it's the leader for a reason.

**Best for:** Plumbing contractors with 5+ technicians, companies with commercial work, businesses serious about scaling, operations needing deep integrations.
