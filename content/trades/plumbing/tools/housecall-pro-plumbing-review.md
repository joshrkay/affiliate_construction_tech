# Housecall Pro Review: Field Service Software for Plumbers

Housecall Pro is a popular all-in-one field service management platform designed specifically for home service businesses—including plumbing contractors. With over 50 integrations and a focus on ease of use, it's a top choice for small to mid-size plumbing companies.

## What Housecall Pro Does

Housecall Pro handles the complete field service workflow:
- Smart scheduling
- Customer management
- Invoicing and payments
- Job tracking
- Photo documentation
- Marketing tools

**Who it's for:** Small to mid-size plumbing contractors, service-focused plumbers, companies wanting professional tools without enterprise complexity.

## Key Features

### Smart Scheduling
- Drag-and-drop calendar
- Technician routing
- Auto-scheduling suggestions
- Customer booking portal
- SMS confirmations and reminders

### Invoicing & Payments
- Create professional invoices
- Send payment links
- Accept credit cards, ACH
- Automatic payment receipts
- Flat-rate pricing templates

### Photo Documentation
- Capture before/after photos
- Add photos to invoices
- Build photo galleries per property
- Useful for warranty claims

### Customer Portal
Your customers get:
- Online booking
- Proposal acceptance
- Invoice payment
- Service history view
- Review requests

### Marketing Tools
- Automated review requests (after completed jobs)
- Email campaigns
- Customer loyalty programs
- Call tracking

## Pros

- **Easy to use** - minimal training required
- **Affordable** - mid-range pricing
- **Mobile app** - full functionality for field techs
- **Professional look** - branded invoices and customer portal
- **Online booking** - customers can self-schedule
- **Photo documentation** - build trust with visual proof
- **Good integrations** - QuickBooks, Stripe, Mailchimp, etc.

## Cons

- **Job costing limited** - not as robust as ServiceTitan
- **Commercial weak** - better for residential
- **Reporting basic** - limited advanced analytics
- **Less scalable** - companies over 20 may outgrow it
- **No native marketing** - add-ons required for some features
- **Customization limited** - fewer workflow options
- **Support varies** - some users report slower response

## Use Cases

### Case 1: Small Plumbing Service
A 3-plumber service company uses Housecall Pro to professionalize operations. They send invoices with photos showing the work, get paid faster with online payments, and collect more reviews.

### Case 2: Growing Plumbing Business
A plumbing company scaling from 2 to 8 technicians uses Housecall Pro to standardize quoting. The flat-rate pricing templates ensure consistent margins across jobs.

### Case 3: Drain Cleaning Specialist
A drain cleaning company uses the photo documentation to show customers the before/after state of pipes, reducing disputes and building trust.

## Verdict

Housecall Pro hits the sweet spot for small to mid-size plumbing contractors. It's more feature-rich than Jobber but more affordable than ServiceTitan, making it ideal for growing plumbing businesses.

**Best for:** Plumbing contractors with 2-15 technicians, residential service businesses, companies wanting professional tools without enterprise costs.

**Skip if:** You need advanced job costing, handle significant commercial work, have more than 20 technicians, or need deep accounting integration.
