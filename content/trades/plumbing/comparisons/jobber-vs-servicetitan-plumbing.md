## Core Features Comparison

### Scheduling & Dispatch

- **Jobber**: Simple drag-and-drop calendar, basic dispatch. Perfect for small teams that need quick scheduling without complexity.
- **ServiceTitan**: Advanced dispatch with route optimization, capacity planning, and real-time technician tracking. Ideal for larger teams managing complex schedules.

### Invoicing & Payments

- **Jobber**: Quick estimate-to-invoice, online payments, basic invoicing templates. Great for small businesses needing fast, professional invoices.
- **ServiceTitan**: Full invoicing suite with job costing, custom templates, automated invoicing, and deep QuickBooks integration. Better for companies needing detailed financial tracking.

### CRM & Marketing

- **Jobber**: Basic CRM with automated follow-ups, review requests, and client portal. Sufficient for small teams.
- **ServiceTitan**: Built-in CRM with marketing automation, membership programs, automated campaigns, and call center features. Better for growing companies wanting to nurture leads.

### Reporting & Analytics

- **Jobber**: Standard reports on jobs, revenue, and basic profitability. Good for small businesses.
- **ServiceTitan**: Advanced dashboards with detailed profitability analysis, technician utilization, and custom reporting. Better for data-driven decision making.

### Integrations

- **Jobber**: QuickBooks, Zapier, Google Calendar, Outlook, Mailchimp.
- **ServiceTitan**: QuickBooks, Sage, Xero, Gusto, Google Calendar, Outlook, Zapier, plus HVAC-specific suppliers. More extensive.

## Pros and Cons

### Jobber

**Pros**

- Intuitive, easy to learn
- Quick invoicing and scheduling

- Automated client communications

**Cons**

- Not as scalable for large teams
- Basic reporting compared to ServiceTitan

### ServiceTitan

**Pros**
- Comprehensive all-in-one platform
- Advanced dispatch and routing
- Strong marketing automation
- Extensive integrations
- Scalable for growth

**Cons**
- Higher price point
- Steeper learning curve
- May be overkill for very small teams

## How to Choose

### Choose Jobber If:
- You have 1-15 technicians
- Budget is a primary concern
- You need simple, intuitive scheduling and invoicing
- You don't require advanced marketing automation
- You're a small-to-mid sized business now but plan to stay lean

### Choose ServiceTitan If:
- You have 10+ technicians (or plan to grow quickly)
- You need advanced dispatch with route optimization
- You want built-in marketing automation and CRM
- You require deep accounting integrations
- You need detailed reporting and analytics

## Conclusion

Both Jobber and ServiceTitan are excellent plumbing field service platforms, but they serve different market segments. Jobber is ideal for small-to-mid sized businesses seeking affordable, intuitive software, while ServiceTitan is better suited for growing companies needing comprehensive features and scalability.

Assess your current team size, budget, and feature needs—and take advantage of free trials to test each platform with your own data before deciding.
