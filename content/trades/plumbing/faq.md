# Plumbing Contractor Software FAQ

Get answers to the most common questions about plumbing contractor software.

## General Questions

### What is plumbing contractor software?
Plumbing contractor software helps plumbers and plumbing companies manage their business operations, including scheduling, dispatch, invoicing, customer management, inventory tracking, and job costing.

### Why do plumbing contractors need software?
Software helps plumbing contractors streamline operations, reduce missed appointments, speed up invoicing, track inventory, and improve customer satisfaction.

### How much does plumbing contractor software cost?
Prices range from $30-$250+ per user per month depending on features. Entry-level tools start around $30-50, mid-tier $60-120, and enterprise $150+.

### Can I try before buying?
Most vendors offer free trials (7-30 days). Take advantage of trials to test with your actual work.

## Scheduling & Dispatch

### Can I schedule recurring service appointments?
Yes—most plumbing software supports recurring appointments for maintenance contracts, filter changes, and annual service agreements.

### How does dispatch work?
Dispatch features let you assign jobs to technicians based on location, skillset, and availability using drag-and-drop calendars.

### Can I track my technicians in real-time?
Many platforms include GPS tracking to see technician locations, optimize routes, and provide customers with accurate arrival times.

## Invoicing & Payments

### Can I create invoices from the field?
Yes—most modern plumbing software lets technicians create and send invoices directly from their mobile app in the field.

### Do you support online payments?
Yes—most platforms integrate with payment processors to accept credit cards, ACH, and digital payments at the point of sale.

### Can I invoice for parts and labor separately?
Yes—most tools let you itemize parts, labor, and other charges on invoices.

## Inventory Management

### Can I track truck inventory?
Yes—many plumbing software platforms include inventory tracking to manage parts on trucks, set reorder alerts, and prevent stockouts.

### Do you support barcode scanning?
Some platforms support barcode scanning for quick parts lookup and inventory management.

## Integrations

### Does plumbing software integrate with accounting?
Yes—most integrate with QuickBooks, Xero, Sage, and other accounting platforms. Always verify specific integrations.

### Can I connect to marketing tools?
Many platforms integrate with Mailchimp, Zapier, and other marketing tools for automated follow-ups and campaigns.

## Getting Started

### How long does implementation take?
Implementation typically takes 2-6 weeks depending on complexity and data migration needs.

### Do I need training?
Most vendors offer free training via webinars, documentation, and videos. Some include dedicated onboarding.

### Can I migrate data from my current system?
Most vendors help migrate data from legacy systems. Check what migration support is included.
