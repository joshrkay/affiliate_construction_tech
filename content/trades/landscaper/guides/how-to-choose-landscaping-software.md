# How to Choose the Right Landscaping Software in 2026

## Step 1: Assess Your Needs

Before choosing software, answer these questions:

1. **How big is your team?** (1-5, 5-20, 20+)
2. **What's your monthly budget?** ($29, $49, $149+)
3. **What features do you need?** (scheduling, CRM, invoicing, etc.)
4. **Do you need mobile access?** (field work requires it)
5. **What integrations do you need?** (QuickBooks, Stripe, etc.)

## Step 2: Set a Budget

- **Under $50/mo:** Jobber, Connecteam
- **$50-100/mo:** Housecall Pro, Buildertrend
- **$150+/mo:** ServiceTitan, Procore

## Step 3: Test Drive

Always use free trials:
- Jobber: 14-day trial
- Housecall Pro: 14-day trial
- ServiceTitan: Custom demo

## Step 4: Check Reviews

Look for:
- User reviews on G2, Capterra
- Real customer testimonials
- Support quality

## Step 5: Make the Decision

Consider:
- Ease of use
- Customer support
- Scalability
- Affiliate program (if you're an affiliate)

## Our Recommendation

For most landscaping businesses: **Jobber**

- Affordable ($29/mo)
- Easy to use
- Great mobile app
- Strong affiliate program
