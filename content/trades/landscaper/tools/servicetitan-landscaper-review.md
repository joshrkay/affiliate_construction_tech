ServiceTitan is an enterprise-grade platform for large landscaping companies. It's feature-rich but comes with a higher price tag.

- **Marketing automation** - email campaigns, callbacks
- **Call recording** - quality assurance
- **Inventory management** - parts tracking
- **Advanced reporting** - business insights

## Cons

- **Expensive** - starts at $149/month
- **Complex** - steep learning curve
- **Overkill** for small businesses

- **Professional:** $249/month
- **Enterprise:** Custom pricing

## Verdict

**ServiceTitan** is best for large landscaping enterprises that need advanced features and have the budget for it.
