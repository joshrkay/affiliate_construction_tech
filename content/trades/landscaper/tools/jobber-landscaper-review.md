# Jobber Review: Field Service Software for Landscaping Companies

Jobber is a field service management platform designed for landscaping companies. With pricing starting at $29/user/month, it's an accessible way for landscapers to professionalize operations.

## What Jobber Does

Jobber handles:
- Scheduling and dispatch
- Customer management
- Quoting and invoicing
- Payment processing
- Route planning

**Who it's for:** Small to mid-size landscaping companies, solo landscapers, lawn care businesses.

## Key Features

### Scheduling
- Drag-and-drop calendar
- Route optimization
- Customer reminders
- Recurring scheduling

### Client Management
- Contact database
- Property notes
- Service history
- Client portal

### Quoting & Invoicing
- Professional quotes
- Convert to jobs
- Online payments
- Recurring invoices

## Pricing

| Plan | Price |
|------|-------|
| Core | $29/user/mo |
| Grow | $49/user/mo |
| Scale | $249/user/mo |

Most landscapers choose **$49/user/month**.

## Pros

- **Affordable** - starts at $29
- **Easy to use** - minimal training
- **Mobile app** - full field functionality
- **Client portal** - online booking

## Cons

- Limited advanced features
- Basic reporting
- Less commercial focus

## Verdict

Jobber is the best choice for small landscaping companies needing professional tools at budget prices.

**Best for:** Small landscaping businesses (1-10 employees), lawn care companies.

**Skip if:** You need enterprise features or commercial-grade job costing.
