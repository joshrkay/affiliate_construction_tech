## Core Features Comparison

### Scheduling & Dispatch

- **ServiceTitan**: Offers advanced drag‑and‑drop scheduling with built‑in routing optimization, capacity planning, and real‑time technician tracking. Great for companies managing 10+ techs and complex schedules.
- **FieldEdge**: Provides a simple, intuitive drag‑and‑drop calendar focused on ease of use. Ideal for smaller teams that need fast scheduling without the learning curve.

### Invoicing & Payments

- **ServiceTitan**: Full invoicing suite with job costing, automated invoicing, online payments, and deep QuickBooks integration. Supports complex pricing structures and custom invoice templates.
- **FieldEdge**: Straightforward invoicing with quick‑estimate‑to‑invoice, online payments, and basic QuickBooks sync. Less sophisticated than ServiceTitan but gets the job done for smaller shops.

### Customer Relationship Management (CRM)

- **ServiceTitan**: Built‑in CRM with marketing automation, membership programs, automated follow‑ups, and call center features. Excellent for growing companies that want to nurture leads and retain customers.
- **FieldEdge**: Basic CRM with customer profiles, service history, and automated review requests. Focused on simplicity rather than advanced marketing.

### Inventory & Parts Tracking

- **ServiceTitan**: Robust parts management with barcode scanning, truck stock tracking, automated reorder alerts, and integration with HVAC parts suppliers.
- **FieldEdge**: Simple inventory tracking with barcode scanning and basic reorder alerts. Sufficient for shops with straightforward inventory needs.

### Reporting & Analytics

- **ServiceTitan**: Advanced dashboards with job profitability, technician utilization, PM compliance rates, and custom reporting. Ideal for data‑driven decision making.
- **FieldEdge**: Standard reports covering job profitability, technician performance, and revenue trends. Less granular but adequate for small‑to‑mid sized businesses.

### Mobile App

- **ServiceTitan**: Comprehensive iOS/Android app with scheduling, dispatch, invoicing, photo capture, signature collection, and office‑side approvals. Technicians and office staff both have robust mobile tools.
- **FieldEdge**: Technician‑focused app with schedule view, photo capture, signature collection, and parts logging. Simple and fast, but fewer office‑side features than ServiceTitan.

### Integrations

- **ServiceTitan**: Deep integrations with QuickBooks (Online & Desktop), Sage, Xero, Gusto (payroll), Google Calendar, Outlook, Zapier, and various HVAC‑specific suppliers. Great for companies that need to connect to a wide ecosystem.
- **FieldEdge**: Core integrations with QuickBooks, Gusto, Google Calendar, Outlook, and Zapier. Less extensive but covers the basics most small‑to‑mid sized shops need.

### Preventive Maintenance (PM)

- **ServiceTitan**: Sophisticated PM module with automated scheduling based on time, mileage, or equipment usage. Supports complex PM contracts and multi‑step templates.
- **FieldEdge**: Simple interval‑based PM scheduling. Good for shops with straightforward PM needs but lacks the advanced conditional logic of ServiceTitan.

## Pros and Cons

### ServiceTitan

**Pros**
- All‑in‑one platform consolidates field service, accounting, job costing, and marketing.
- Advanced routing optimization saves time and fuel.
- Strong marketing and membership features help grow recurring revenue.
- Extensive integration ecosystem.
- Scalable for growing companies.

**Cons**
- Higher price point—may be overkill for very small shops.
- Steeper learning curve due to feature richness.
- Requires more setup and configuration time.

### FieldEdge

**Pros**
- Lower cost, ideal for small‑to‑mid sized HVAC businesses.
- Intuitive, easy‑to‑learn interface.
- Fast invoicing and straightforward dispatch.
- Good mobile app for field technicians.
- More affordable entry point.

**Cons**
- Fewer advanced features (e.g., no built‑in job costing, limited marketing automation).
- Less sophisticated routing optimization.
- Smaller ecosystem of third‑party integrations.
- May require additional tools for advanced accounting needs.

## How to Choose

### Choose ServiceTitan if:
- You have 10+ technicians and need an all‑in‑one solution.
- You want deep accounting, job costing, and marketing automation.
- You need advanced routing optimization and capacity planning.
- You’re willing to invest more for a scalable platform.
- You want extensive third‑party integrations.

### Choose FieldEdge if:
- You have 1‑20 technicians and need a simple, affordable solution.
- You’re focused primarily on dispatch and invoicing.
- You prefer an intuitive, easy‑to‑learn interface.
- You have a tight budget and don’t need advanced accounting or marketing features.
- You want a fast setup with minimal configuration.
