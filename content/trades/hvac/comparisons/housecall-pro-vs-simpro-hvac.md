## Core Features Comparison

### Scheduling & Dispatch

- **Housecall Pro**: Simple drag-and-drop calendar, perfect for small teams needing quick scheduling.
- **Simpro**: Advanced scheduling with job costing, better for complex operations.

### Invoicing & Payments

- **Housecall Pro**: Fast estimate-to-invoice, online payments, basic invoicing templates.
- **Simpro**: Full invoicing with detailed job costing, customizable templates.

### CRM & Marketing

- **Housecall Pro**: Basic CRM with automated follow-ups and review requests.
- **Simpro**: Built-in CRM with project management and marketing automation.

### Reporting

- **Housecall Pro**: Standard reports on jobs, revenue, and basic profitability.
- **Simpro**: Advanced reporting with detailed job costing and profitability analysis.
