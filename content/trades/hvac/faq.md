# HVAC Contractor Software FAQ

Get answers to the most common questions about HVAC contractor software.

## General Questions

### What is HVAC contractor software?
HVAC contractor software helps heating, ventilation, and air conditioning companies manage service calls, installations, preventive maintenance, invoicing, customer relationships, and field operations.

### Why do HVAC contractors need software?
Software helps HVAC contractors manage scheduling, reduce missed preventive maintenance appointments, streamline invoicing, track inventory, and grow their business through better customer communication.

### How much does HVAC contractor software cost?
Prices range from $40-$250+ per technician per month depending on features. Entry-level starts around $40-80, mid-tier $80-150, and enterprise $150+.

### Can I try before buying?
Most vendors offer free trials (7-30 days). Test with your actual work before committing.

## Preventive Maintenance

### Can I schedule preventive maintenance automatically?
Yes—most HVAC software supports automated PM scheduling based on time intervals, equipment usage, or customer agreements.

### How do I track equipment service history?
Most platforms include equipment profiles that track service history, repairs, and maintenance for each customer's HVAC system.

### Can I send automated reminders to customers?
Yes—most tools send automated text, email, or voice reminders before scheduled appointments to reduce no-shows.

## Scheduling & Dispatch

### Can I optimize routes for my technicians?
Many platforms include route optimization to reduce drive time, fuel costs, and improve efficiency.

### Can technicians update job status from the field?
Yes—mobile apps let technicians update job status, capture photos, collect signatures, and log parts used in real-time.

### Can I track technician locations?
Many platforms include GPS tracking to see technician locations and provide customers with accurate arrival times.

## Invoicing & Payments

### Can I create invoices from the field?
Yes—most modern HVAC software lets technicians create and send invoices directly from their mobile device.

### Do you support online payments?
Yes—most integrate with payment processors to accept credit cards, ACH, and digital payments.

### Can I handle service agreements and memberships?
Yes—many platforms support recurring service agreements, maintenance memberships, and automated billing.

## Integrations

### Does HVAC software integrate with accounting?
Yes—most integrate with QuickBooks, Xero, Sage, and other accounting platforms.

### Can I connect to smart thermostats?
Some platforms integrate with Nest, Ecobee, and other smart devices for remote diagnostics and monitoring.

## Getting Started

### How long does implementation take?
Implementation typically takes 2-8 weeks depending on complexity and data migration needs.

### Do I need training?
Most vendors offer free training via webinars, documentation, and videos. Some include dedicated onboarding.

### Can I migrate data from my current system?
Most vendors help migrate data from legacy systems. Check what migration support is included.
