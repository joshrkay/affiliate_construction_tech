# ServiceTitan Review: The Gold Standard for HVAC Contractors

ServiceTitan is the most comprehensive field service management platform available for HVAC contractors. With over 70 integrations, real-time job costing, and enterprise-grade features, it's the choice of many of the largest HVAC companies in North America.

## What ServiceTitan Does

ServiceTitan handles every aspect of running an HVAC business:
- Scheduling and dispatch
- Customer relationship management
- Invoicing and payments
- Job costing and analytics
- Marketing automation
- Commercial project management

**Who it's for:** Mid-to-large HVAC contractors, service businesses with 5+ technicians, companies seeking to scale.

## Key Features

### Advanced Scheduling
- Drag-and-drop dispatch board
- Technician availability and skills matching
- Auto-scheduling algorithms
- Route optimization
- Real-time GPS tracking

### Real-Time Job Costing
- Track labor costs vs. estimate in real-time
- See profit margins per job instantly
- Historical job cost analysis
- Predictive pricing insights
- Materials tracking

### 70+ Integrations
HVAC-specific integrations include:
- QuickBooks Online & Desktop
- Sage
- XOi (field documentation)
- ServiceChannel
- Honeywell
- Aprilaire

### Marketing Suite
- Automated review requests
- Email campaigns
- Call tracking
- Customer portal with online booking

### Commercial Capabilities
- Project-based tracking
- Change order management
- Retention tracking
- Multiple location support

## Pricing

ServiceTitan doesn't publish pricing. Expect to budget **$75-$150/user/month**.

Implementation typically costs $5,000-$15,000 additional.

## Pros

- **Comprehensive** - every feature you need
- **Real-time visibility** - know profit per job instantly
- **Strong commercial support** - handles big jobs
- **Best integrations** - 70+ connections
- **Proven at scale** - used by major HVAC companies
- **Marketing built-in** - generate leads
- **Industry leader** - well-established

## Cons

- **Expensive** - premium pricing plus implementation fees
- **Complex** - steep learning curve
- **Sales process** - no self-serve
- **Setup time** - 4-8 weeks
- **Overkill for small ops** - too much tool for 1-5 techs

## Verdict

ServiceTitan is the gold standard for HVAC contractors who need enterprise-grade features. It's expensive and complex, but if you're serious about scaling, it's the leader for a reason.

**Best for:** HVAC contractors with 5+ technicians, commercial-focused businesses, companies serious about scaling.

**Skip if:** You're small (1-4 techs) or you only need basic scheduling.
