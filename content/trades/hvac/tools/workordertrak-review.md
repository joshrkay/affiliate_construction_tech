# WorkOrderTrak Review: Field Service Software

WorkOrderTrak is field service management software designed for contractors and service businesses.

## What WorkOrderTrak Does

WorkOrderTrak handles:
- Work order management
- Scheduling
- Customer database
- Accounting integration
- Mobile access

## Key Features

### Work Orders
- Create/assign
- Track status
- History
- Notes

### Scheduling
- Calendar view
- Technician scheduling
- Availability

### Customer Database
- Contact info
- Service history
- Property details
- Notes

## Pricing

$49-$99/user/month

## Pros

- Affordable
- Easy to use
- Mobile app
- Good for small teams

## Cons

- Limited advanced features
- Smaller company
- Less integrations

## Verdict

WorkOrderTrak is a solid budget option for small contractors needing basic field service management.
