# FieldEdge Review: Trade-Specific Field Service Software

FieldEdge is a field service management platform designed specifically for HVAC, electrical, and plumbing contractors. Unlike general-purpose tools, FieldEdge is built from the ground up for trade businesses.

## What FieldEdge Does

FieldEdge handles:
- Dispatching
- Service agreements
- Invoicing
- Customer history
- Mobile access
- Service agreements

**Who it's for:** HVAC, electrical, and plumbing contractors wanting trade-specific features.

## Key Features

### Trade-Specific Design
Built specifically for trades:
- HVAC-specific workflows
- Electrical service tracking
- Plumbing service modules

### Dispatching
- Visual dispatch board
- Technician scheduling
- Customer notifications
- Route views

### Service Agreements
- Maintenance contracts
- Recurring service
- Agreement tracking
- Expiration alerts

### Customer History
- Property database
- Service history
- Equipment tracking
- Notes and documents

### Mobile Access
- Field app
- Time tracking
- Photo capture
- Signature collection

## Pricing

FieldEdge doesn't publish pricing. Expect **$50-$100/user/month** based on features.

Contact sales for exact pricing.

## Pros

- **Trade-specific** - built for trades
- **Service agreements** - contract management
- **Customer history** - property tracking
- **Mobile app** - full field functionality
- **Industry focus** - understands trade needs

## Cons

- **Pricing not transparent** - needs sales call
- **Limited integrations** - fewer than competitors
- **Smaller company** - less established
- **Scaling limited** - may outgrow

## Verdict

FieldEdge is a solid choice for trade contractors who want software built specifically for their industry.

**Best for:** HVAC, electrical, plumbing contractors wanting trade-specific features.

**Skip if:** You need many integrations or enterprise scale.
