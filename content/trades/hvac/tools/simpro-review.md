# SimPRO Review: Field Service Management for Contractors

SimPRO is a comprehensive field service management platform designed for trade contractors. With strong job management and scheduling features, it's popular with HVAC, electrical, and service companies.

## What SimPRO Does

SimPRO handles:
- Job management
- Scheduling
- Quoting
- Invoicing
- Inventory management
- Job costing

**Who it's for:** Trade contractors, service businesses, companies needing inventory tracking.

## Key Features

### Job Management
- Work orders
- Job tracking
- Status updates
- Customer info
- Multi-tech jobs

### Scheduling
- Calendar view
- Technician scheduling
- Drag-and-drop
- Availability tracking

### Quoting
- Professional quotes
- Templates
- Convert to jobs
- Pricing tables

### Inventory Management
- Parts tracking
- Warehouse management
- Purchase orders
- Stock levels
- Cost tracking

### Job Costing
- Labor tracking
- Materials costing
- Profit margins
- Reporting

## Pricing

SimPRO doesn't publish pricing. Expect **$75-$150/user/month**.

Contact sales for quote.

## Pros

- **Inventory management** - strong parts tracking
- **Job costing** - good margin visibility
- **Scheduling** - comprehensive
- **Trade-focused** - built for contractors

## Cons

- **Pricing not transparent**
- **Learning curve**
- **Limited marketing**
- **Fewer integrations**

## Verdict

SimPRO is a strong choice for contractors who need inventory management and job costing.

**Best for:** Trade contractors needing inventory tracking, service companies.

**Skip if:** You need marketing features or have limited IT resources.
