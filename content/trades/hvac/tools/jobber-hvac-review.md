# Jobber Review: Field Service Software for Small HVAC Contractors

Jobber is a field service management platform designed for small to mid-size service businesses—including HVAC contractors. With pricing starting at just $29/user/month, it's one of the most accessible options for HVAC technicians just starting to digitize their operations.

## What Jobber Does

Jobber handles the essentials:
- Scheduling and dispatch
- Customer management
- Quoting and invoicing
- Payment processing
- Mobile access

**Who it's for:** Small HVAC contractors (1-10 employees), solo technicians, service businesses needing professional tools at budget prices.

## Key Features

### Scheduling
- Drag-and-drop calendar
- Technician availability tracking
- Route views for the day
- Client notifications

### Client Management
- Contact database
- Service history
- Client portal for online booking
- Notes per property

### Quoting & Invoicing
- Create professional quotes
- Convert quotes to jobs
- Online invoice acceptance
- Recurring invoicing

### Mobile App
- View schedule
- Capture photos
- Collect signatures
- Process payments on-site

## Pricing

| Plan | Price | Who it's for |
|------|-------|--------------|
| Core | $29/user/mo | Basic scheduling |
| Grow | $49/user/mo | + quoting + payments |
| Scale | $249/user/mo | + advanced automation |

Most small HVAC contractors land on the **Grow plan at $49/user/month**.

## Pros

- **Affordable** - starts at $29/user/month
- **Easy to use** - minimal training required
- **Mobile app** - full functionality in the field
- **Client portal** - online booking
- **Quick setup** - days not months
- **80+ integrations**

## Cons

- **Limited advanced features** - no complex job costing
- **Less commercial focus** - better for residential
- **Reporting basic** - limited analytics
- **Scaling challenges** - may outgrow at 20+ techs

## Verdict

Jobber is the sweet spot for small HVAC contractors who need professional tools without enterprise complexity or cost.

**Best for:** Small HVAC contractors (1-10 employees), solo technicians, service-focused businesses.

**Skip if:** You need advanced job costing, have 20+ technicians, or need commercial-grade features.
