# ServiceTitan Review: Complete Field Service for Electrical Contractors

ServiceTitan is an all-in-one field service management platform designed for home and commercial service businesses—including electrical contractors. With 70+ integrations and a comprehensive feature set, it's become one of the most popular choices for electrical companies that have outgrown basic job tracking.

## What ServiceTitan Does

ServiceTitan handles the entire field service lifecycle: scheduling, dispatch, customer management, invoicing, job costing, and marketing. It's a true all-in-one platform, meaning you can run your entire electrical business from within ServiceTitan rather than piecing together multiple tools.

**Who it's for:** Mid-to-large electrical contractors, electrical companies looking to scale, service businesses needing comprehensive features.

## Key Features

### Scheduling & Dispatch
ServiceTitan's scheduling tools are robust:
- Drag-and-drop calendar
- Auto-scheduling based on technician availability
- Route optimization
- Real-time technician tracking
- Customer arrival notifications

### Job Costing & Real-Time Insights
One of ServiceTitan's strengths is its costing capabilities:
- Track actual vs. estimated time
- Real-time profit visibility
- Predictive insights for pricing
- Job-level profitability reports

### 70+ Integrations
ServiceTitan connects with:
- QuickBooks Online & Desktop
- Sage
- XOi (field documentation)
- ServiceChannel
- Viewpoint Spectrum
- And 60+ more

### Marketing Suite
Built-in marketing tools include:
- Customer review requests
- Email campaigns
- Neighborhood campaigns (door-knocker tracking)
- Website integration

### Customer Portal
Customers can book online, sign proposals, and pay invoices through a branded customer portal.

## Pricing

ServiceTitan doesn't publish pricing. Expect to budget **$50-$150/user/month** for most electrical contractors.

**Factors affecting price:**
- Number of users
- Features included
- Add-ons (marketing, customer portal)
- Implementation and training

Most contractors need a sales call to get an accurate quote. Implementation typically takes 4-8 weeks.

## Pros

- **Comprehensive** - handles every aspect of field service
- **Strong integrations** - 70+ connections to other software
- **Real-time costing** - know profit margins per job instantly
- **Marketing built-in** - generate reviews and leads
- **Commercial focus** - handles larger jobs well
- **Proven at scale** - used by major service companies

## Cons

- **Expensive** - premium pricing, setup fees
- **Complex** - steep learning curve
- **Requires commitment** - minimum user counts may apply
- **Support varies** - some users report slow support
- **Overkill for small ops** - too much tool if you have 1-2 technicians
- **Sales process** - no self-serve signup

A 15-technician electrical service company transitions from QuickBooks + spreadsheets to ServiceTitan. They reduce administrative time by 12 hours/week and start tracking actual job costs, discovering that some service types were losing money.

## Verdict

ServiceTitan is the enterprise choice for electrical contractors who need comprehensive field service management. It's not cheap, and it's not simple—but if you're serious about scaling and need every feature under one roof, ServiceTitan delivers.

**Best for:** Mid-to-large electrical contractors, companies with 5+ technicians, businesses needing commercial-grade features.
