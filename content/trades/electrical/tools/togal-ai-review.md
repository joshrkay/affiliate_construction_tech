# Togal.AI Review: AI-Powered Takeoff for Electrical Contractors

Togal.AI is a cloud-based takeoff software that uses artificial intelligence to automatically detect, measure, and label electrical components on architectural plans. For electrical contractors tired of spending hours manually measuring conduit runs, panel schedules, and lighting layouts, Togol.AI promises to cut that time by 80%.

## What Togal.AI Does

Togal.AI specializes in AI-powered digital takeoffs. Upload a PDF set of plans, and the software automatically identifies and measures electrical components. It's designed specifically for electrical contractors who need to speed up their pre-construction workflow without sacrificing accuracy.

**Who it's for:** Electrical estimators, pre-construction managers, and electrical contractors who do significant takeoff work.

## Key Features

### AI-Powered Auto-Detection
Togal.AI's claim to fame is its ability to automatically detect components on floor plans. The AI recognizes:
- Electrical panels
- Lighting fixtures
- Receptacles and outlets
- Conduit runs
- Switches and dimmers
- HVAC equipment (for coordination)

The system achieves 98% accuracy on standard floor plans, according to their marketing.

### Cloud Collaboration
Because it's cloud-based, multiple team members can work on the same takeoff simultaneously. This is crucial for larger electrical firms where one person might do the rough-in takeoff while another handles the fixture count.

### Togal.CHAT
The standout feature is the chat interface—you can literally ask the AI questions about the plans. "Show me all 20-amp circuits" or "Highlight all lighting on the second floor" and the AI responds visually.

### Drawing Comparison
Compare two versions of a set and see exactly what changed between them. This is invaluable for addendum work or value engineering options.

## Pricing

Togal.AI doesn't publish pricing publicly. You'll need to contact their sales team for a quote. Based on industry positioning, expect to budget **$200-$500/month** for a typical electrical contractor.

**What to expect:**
- Setup and training typically included
- Per-seat or flat-fee options available
- Free demo to test with your actual plans

## Pros

- **80% faster takeoffs** reported by users
- **98% accuracy** claim on standard plans
- **Real collaboration** without version conflicts
- **AI chat** makes finding information intuitive
- **Drawing comparison** saves hours on addenda

## Cons

- **No public pricing** - requires sales call
- **Learning curve** - AI doesn't catch everything initially
- **Accuracy drops** on poor-quality PDFs or unusual symbol sets
- **Limited accounting integration** compared to full-suite tools
- **Requires internet** - not great for remote sites with poor connectivity

## Use Cases

### Case 1: Commercial Electrical Estimator
A mid-size electrical contractor bidding on a 50,000 sq ft medical office building uses Togal.AI to handle the initial takeoff. What previously took 16 hours of manual measurement now takes about 3 hours, giving estimators more time to focus on pricing strategy and value engineering.

### Case 2: Growing Electrical Firm
An electrical company scaling from $2M to $10M in annual revenue adopts Togal.AI to standardize their takeoff process across multiple project managers. The cloud collaboration ensures everyone uses the same methodology.

### Case 3: Design-Build Electrical Contractor
A design-build firm uses Togal.CHAT to quickly extract quantities for preliminary budgets during the design phase, allowing faster client feedback on scope decisions.

## Verdict

Togal.AI is the real deal for electrical contractors who takeoffs are a bottleneck. The AI technology genuinely speeds things up, and the chat feature is genuinely useful. The main drawback is the lack of transparent pricing and the fact that you need decent internet to use it.

**Best for:** Electrical contractors and estimators who want to dramatically speed up their takeoff process and don't mind the sales call for pricing.

**Skip if:** You work primarily offline, have very simple takeoffs, or need full accounting/PM integration in the same tool.
