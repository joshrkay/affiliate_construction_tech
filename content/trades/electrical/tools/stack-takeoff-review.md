# STACK Takeoff Review: Cloud-Based Digital Takeoff

STACK is a cloud-based takeoff and estimating platform that helps contractors measure plans and create estimates digitally. It's popular for electrical, HVAC, and general contracting takeoffs.

## What STACK Does

STACK handles:
- Digital takeoff from PDF
- Cloud storage
- Collaboration
- Estimating
- Integration

**Who it's for:** Contractors who do significant takeoff work, estimators, pre-construction teams.

## Key Features

### Digital Takeoff
- PDF plan measurement
- Symbol library
- Counting tools
- Area measurement
- Length measurement

### Cloud Storage
- Save plans online
- Access anywhere
- Version control
- Team sharing

### Collaboration
- Multiple users
- Real-time updates
- Comments
- Permissions

### Estimating
- Assembly library
- Pricing integration
- Bid summaries
- Export to Excel

### Integrations
- QuickBooks
- Excel
- CSV export

## Pricing

| Plan | Price |
|------|-------|
| Starter | $199/month |

Annual billing saves ~20%.

## Pros

- **Cloud-based** - access anywhere
- **Collaboration** - team features
- **No software install** - browser-based
- **Good support**
- **Easy to learn**

## Cons

- **No AI** - manual measurement
- **Limited advanced features**
- **Accuracy depends on user**
- **Internet required**

## Verdict

STACK is a solid choice for contractors who need reliable cloud-based takeoff without the enterprise price tag.

**Best for:** Contractors doing takeoff, small to mid-size estimating teams.

**Skip if:** You need AI-powered features or work offline frequently.
