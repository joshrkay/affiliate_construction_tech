# Best Electrical Contractor Software (2026)

Electrical contractors need the right software to manage jobs, customers, and growing businesses. Here's our comprehensive guide to the best options.

## Best for Estimating: Togal.AI

AI-powered takeoff software that automatically detects components on plans. 80% faster takeoffs.

**Best for:** Contractors who do significant takeoff work

## Best Overall Field Service: ServiceTitan

Comprehensive field service management with real-time job costing and 70+ integrations.

**Best for:** Growing electrical companies

## Best for Small Business: Jobber

Affordable and easy to use. Perfect for small electrical teams.

**Best for:** Small electrical contractors (1-10)

## Best for Takeoff: STACK

Cloud-based takeoff and estimating. Collaborate with your team.

**Best for:** Contractors doing digital takeoffs

## Best Desktop Option: PlanSwift

Traditional desktop takeoff software. One-time purchase.

**Best for:** Office-based estimators

## How to Choose

Consider:
- Type of work (service vs construction)
- Company size
- Budget
- Takeoff needs
- Integration requirements
