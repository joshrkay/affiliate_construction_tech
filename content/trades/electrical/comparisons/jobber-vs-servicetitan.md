# Jobber vs ServiceTitan: Complete Comparison

Choosing between Jobber and ServiceTitan is one of the most common decisions for field service contractors. Both are industry leaders, but they serve different business sizes and needs. Here's the complete comparison.

## Quick Summary

| Factor | Jobber | ServiceTitan |
|--------|--------|--------------|
| **Price** | $29-$249/mo | $75-$150/mo |
| **Best For** | Small biz | Mid-large |
| **Ease** | Very easy | Moderate |
| **Job Costing** | Basic | Advanced |
| **Integrations** | 80+ | 70+ |

## Pricing Comparison

### Jobber
- **Core:** $29/user/month
- **Grow:** $49/user/month  
- **Scale:** $249/user/month
- Free 14-day trial
- No implementation fees

### ServiceTitan
- **Estimated:** $75-$150/user/month
- Implementation: $5,000-$15,000
- Requires sales call
- Minimum user counts may apply

**Winner:** Jobber for budget-conscious small businesses

## Features Comparison

### Scheduling
- **Jobber:** Easy drag-drop, good for small teams
- **ServiceTitan:** Advanced dispatch board, optimization, GPS tracking

### Job Costing
- **Jobber:** Basic tracking
- **ServiceTitan:** Real-time labor/material tracking, predictive insights

### Integrations
- **Jobber:** 80+ connections
- **ServiceTitan:** 70+ connections, more enterprise-focused

### Marketing
- **Jobber:** Add-on
- **ServiceTitan:** Built-in

**Winner:** ServiceTitan for features, Jobber for simplicity

## Best For

### Choose Jobber If:
- You have 1-10 technicians
- Budget is a primary concern
- You need simple, easy-to-use software
- You're just starting to digitize
- Residential focus

### Choose ServiceTitan If:
- You have 5+ technicians
- You need advanced job costing
- Commercial work is significant
- You want marketing built-in
- You're serious about scaling

## Real-World Scenarios

### Small Electrical Service (2 techs)
**Recommendation: Jobber**
At $49/user/month = $98 total. Covers scheduling, invoicing, basic tracking. ServiceTitan would be overkill.

### Growing HVAC Company (12 techs)
**Recommendation: ServiceTitan**
At $100/user/month = $1,200/month. Worth it for real-time costing, 70+ integrations, marketing automation.

### Small Painting Business (4 painters)
**Recommendation: Jobber**
Simple needs, budget-friendly, easy for team to adopt.

### Commercial Mechanical Contractor (25+ techs)
**Recommendation: ServiceTitan**
Enterprise features, commercial focus, scaling capabilities.

## Verdict

**Jobber wins for small businesses** that need professional tools at affordable prices.

**ServiceTitan wins for growing companies** that need enterprise-grade features and are willing to pay for them.

The choice comes down to:
1. Company size
2. Budget
3. Feature needs
4. Growth plans
