# Togal.AI vs STACK Takeoff: Complete Comparison

Togal.AI and STACK are two popular takeoff solutions—but they're fundamentally different. Togal.AI uses AI while STACK is traditional manual takeoff. Here's the comparison.

## Quick Summary

| Factor | Togal.AI | STACK |
|--------|----------|-------|
| **AI Detection** | Yes | No |
| **Cloud** | Yes | Yes |
| **Price** | $200-$500/mo | $199-$499/mo |
| **Chat Interface** | Yes | No |

## Key Differences

### Togal.AI (AI-Powered)
- **Auto-detection** of components
- **Chat with your plans**
- **Drawing comparison**
- **98% accuracy claim**
- **Faster takeoffs**

### STACK (Traditional)
- **Manual measurement**
- **Symbol libraries**
- **Count tools**
- **Assembly support**
- **No AI**

## Pricing

### Togal.AI
- Contact sales for pricing
- Estimated: $200-$500/month
- No public pricing

### STACK
- Starter: $199/month
- Professional: $349/month
- Enterprise: $499/month

**Winner:** STACK for transparent pricing

## Best For

### Choose Togal.AI If:
- You want AI automation
- Speed is critical
- You ask questions about plans
- You do many takeoffs
- Modern tech appeals to you

### Choose STACK If:
- You prefer manual control
- Transparent pricing matters
- Budget is a concern
- You work offline sometimes
- Simpler tool works for you

## Verdict

**Togal.AI wins for contractors** who want to dramatically speed up takeoffs with AI.

**STACK wins for contractors** who prefer traditional tools or need transparent pricing.

The choice is really about whether you want AI or not.
