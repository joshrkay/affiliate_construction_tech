# ServiceTitan Review: Field Service Software for Roofing Contractors

ServiceTitan is the most comprehensive field service management platform for roofing contractors. With powerful scheduling, customer management, and job costing tools, it's the choice of growing roofing companies ready to scale.

## What ServiceTitan Does

ServiceTitan handles:
- Smart scheduling and dispatch
- Customer relationship management
- Invoicing and payments
- Job costing and analytics
- Marketing automation

**Who it's for:** Mid-to-large roofing contractors, growing roofing businesses, companies handling both residential and commercial work.

## Key Features

### Scheduling & Dispatch
- Visual dispatch board
- Technician routing
- Skills-based scheduling
- Customer notifications

### Job Costing
- Track labor vs. estimate in real-time
- Materials tracking
- Profit margins per job
- Historical analysis

### 70+ Integrations
- QuickBooks
- Sage
- XOi
- Call tracking tools

### Marketing
- Automated review requests
- Email campaigns
- Customer portal

## Pricing

Expect to budget **$75-$150/user/month**. Contact sales for exact pricing.

## Pros

- **Comprehensive** - every feature
- **Real-time visibility** - know profit per job
- **Strong integrations** - 70+ connections
- **Marketing built-in** - generate leads
- **Scalable** - grows with you

## Cons

- **Expensive** - premium pricing
- **Complex** - learning curve
- **Sales required** - no self-serve
- **Overkill** - too much for small ops

## Verdict

ServiceTitan is the gold standard for roofing contractors who need enterprise-grade features.

**Best for:** Roofing contractors with 5+ technicians, growing companies.

**Skip if:** You're small (1-4 roofers) or only need basic scheduling.
