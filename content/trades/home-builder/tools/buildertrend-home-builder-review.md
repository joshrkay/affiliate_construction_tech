# Buildertrend Review: Construction Management for Home Builders

Buildertrend is the leading construction management software for home builders and remodelers. With over 10,000 builder customers, it's the residential construction industry standard.

## What Buildertrend Does

Buildertrend handles:
- Project management
- Scheduling
- Client communication
- Selections management
- Budget tracking
- Subcontractor management
- Warranty tracking

## Key Features

### Project Management
- Task lists and to-dos
- Change orders
- Document storage
- Photo documentation
- Daily logs

### Client Communication
- Client portal
- Selection meetings
- Communication log
- Approvals
- Transparency

### Scheduling
- Gantt charts
- Phase scheduling
- Subcontractor coordination
- Client-facing schedule

### Financial Tracking
- Budget vs actual
- Cost tracking
- Progress billing
- Lien waivers

## Pros

- **Designed for home building** - fits residential perfectly
- **Client portal** - customers love it
- **Selection management** - track upgrades/allowances
- **Easy to use** - intuitive
- **Good support** - responsive
- **Strong integrations** - QuickBooks, etc.

## Cons

- **Limited commercial** - better for residential
- **Less enterprise** - may outgrow
- **Reporting basic** - limited analytics

## Use Cases

### Custom Home Builder
A custom home builder manages 4-6 homes simultaneously using Buildertrend. They track selections, schedules, and budgets in one place, reducing client questions by 50%.

### Remodeling Contractor
A remodeling company uses the client portal to keep homeowners informed during renovations, increasing satisfaction and referrals.

### Small GC
A small GC transitions from spreadsheets to Buildertrend, professionalizing operations and tracking job costs.

## Verdict

Buildertrend is the best choice for home builders and remodelers who want comprehensive features without enterprise complexity or cost.

**Best for:** Home builders, remodelers, small GCs, design-build firms, companies with 1-20 projects.

**Skip if:** You primarily do commercial work or need enterprise features.
