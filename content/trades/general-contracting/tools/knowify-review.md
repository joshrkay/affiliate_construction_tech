# Knowify Review: Construction Job Costing and Accounting

Knowify is job costing and accounting software designed specifically for construction companies. It's popular with specialty contractors who need better financial visibility than QuickBooks provides.

## What Knowify Does

Knowify handles:
- Job costing
- Time tracking
- Expense management
- Financial reporting
- Integrations

## Key Features

### Job Costing
- Track labor by employee
- Materials tracking
- Subcontractor costs
- Profit per job
- Budget vs actual

### Time Tracking
- Employee time sheets
- GPS tracking
- Labor allocation
- Payroll integration

### Financials
- P&L by job
- Revenue recognition
- Balance sheet
- Cash flow

## Pricing

| Plan | Price |
|------|-------|
| Starter | $99/month |
| Professional | $299/month |
| Premier | $599/month |

## Pros

- Construction-specific
- Good job costing
- Integrates with QuickBooks
- Reasonable price

## Cons

- Less established
- Limited features vs enterprise
- Smaller user base

## Verdict

Knowify is excellent for contractors who need construction-specific job costing without enterprise costs.
