# CoConstruct Review: Construction Management for Builders

CoConstruct is a cloud-based construction management platform designed specifically for custom home builders and remodelers. It combines project management, client communication, and financial tracking in one platform.

## What CoConstruct Does

CoConstruct handles:
- Project management
- Client communication
- Selection management
- Scheduling
- Budget tracking
- Change orders
- Warranty tracking

**Who it's for:** Custom home builders, high-end remodelers, design-build firms.

## Key Features

### Project Management
- Task tracking
- To-do lists
- Timeline management
- Document storage
- Photo documentation

### Client Communication
- Client portal
- Selection meetings
- Communication log
- Approvals
- Transparency

### Selection Management
- Track selections
- Allowances vs. upgrades
- Client choices
- Pricing impacts

### Scheduling
- Phase scheduling
- Subcontractor coordination
- Client-facing schedule
- Reminders

### Financial Tracking
- Budget vs. actual
- Change orders
- Selections tracking
- Profitability

## Pricing

CoConstruct pricing is tiered:

| Plan | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| Standard | $15/user/mo | Basic PM |
| Plus | $25/user/mo | + Client tools |
| Complete | $35/user/mo | Everything |

Plus setup fee. Annual billing.

## Pros

- **Builder-focused** - designed for home building
- **Client communication** - excellent portal
- **Selection management** - track upgrades
- **Easy to use** - intuitive
- **Good support**

## Cons

- **Limited integrations**
- **Less commercial**
- **Reporting basic**
- **Can be pricey**

## Verdict

CoConstruct is an excellent choice for custom home builders and remodelers who prioritize client communication and selection tracking.

**Best for:** Custom home builders, high-end remodelers, design-build firms.

**Skip if:** You do commercial work or need deep integrations.
