# Foundation Software Review: Construction Accounting

Foundation is enterprise-grade construction accounting software designed for contractors with complex financial needs.

## What Foundation Does

Foundation handles:
- Job costing
- Progress billing
- Payroll
- Equipment tracking
- Financial reporting

## Key Features

### Job Costing
- Track labor, materials, subs
- Cost codes
- Budget vs actual
- Profitability by job

### Progress Billing
- AIA billing
- Retention tracking
- Change orders
- Lien waivers

### Equipment
- Equipment costing
- Depreciation
- Utilization tracking

## Pricing

Custom pricing - contact for quote.

## Pros

- Construction-specific
- Comprehensive
- Strong reporting
- Industry-specific

## Cons

- Expensive
- Complex
- Dated UI

## Verdict

Foundation is for contractors with complex accounting needs who have outgrown QuickBooks.
