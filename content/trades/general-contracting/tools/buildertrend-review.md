# Buildertrend Review: Construction Management for Small Contractors

Buildertrend is a cloud-based construction management platform designed specifically for home builders, remodelers, and small general contractors. It's more approachable than Procore while still offering comprehensive features.

## What Buildertrend Does

Buildertrend handles:
- Project management
- Scheduling
- Customer management
- Financial tracking
- Subcontractor management
- Document storage

**Who it's for:** Home builders, remodelers, small GCs, design-build firms.

## Key Features

### Project Management
- Task lists and scheduling
- To-do items
- Change orders
- Selections/allowances
- Warranty tracking

### Scheduling
- Gantt charts
- Phase scheduling
- Subcontractor scheduling
- Client-facing schedule

### Customer Management
- Lead tracking
- Client portal
- Communication log
- Selection choices

### Financial Tracking
- Budget vs. actual
- Cost tracking
- Progress billing
- Lien waivers
- QuickBooks integration

### Subcontractor Management
- Sub contracts
- Scope of work
- Submittals
- Insurance tracking

## Pricing

Buildertrend pricing is straightforward:

| Plan | Price | Who's it for |
|------|-------|--------------|
| Base | $10/user/mo | Basic needs |
| Classic | $15/user/mo | Most builders |
| Complete | $20/user/mo | Full features |

Plus setup fee. Monthly or annual billing.

## Pros

- **Designed for home building** - fits residential perfectly
- **Client portal** - customers love it
- **Easy to use** - less training than Procore
- **Affordable** - much cheaper than enterprise
- **Strong integrations** - QuickBooks, etc.
- **Good support** - responsive team

## Cons

- **Less commercial** - better for residential
- **Limited enterprise** - may outgrow
- **Reporting basic** - limited analytics
- **Less flexible** - less customization than Procore

## Use Cases

### Case 1: Custom Home Builder
A custom home builder uses Buildertrend to manage 4-6 homes under construction simultaneously, tracking selections, schedules, and budgets in one place.

### Case 2: Remodeling Contractor
A remodeling company uses Buildertrend to keep clients informed through the client portal, reducing phone calls while increasing client satisfaction.

### Case 3: Small GC
A small GC transitioning from spreadsheets uses Buildertrend to professionalize operations and track job costs.

## How It Compares

| Feature | Buildertrend | Procore | CoConstruct |
|---------|--------------|---------|-------------|
| Price | $10-$20/user | $15-$50/user | $15-$25/user |
| Ease | Easy | Complex | Moderate |
| Residential | Excellent | Good | Excellent |
| Commercial | Limited | Excellent | Limited |
| Client Portal | Yes | Limited | Yes |

## Verdict

Buildertrend is the best choice for home builders and remodelers who want comprehensive features without enterprise complexity or cost.

**Best for:** Home builders, remodelers, small GCs, design-build firms, companies with 1-20 projects.

**Skip if:** You primarily do commercial work, need enterprise features, or manage 50+ concurrent projects.
