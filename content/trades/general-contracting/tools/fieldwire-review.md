# FieldWire Review: Construction Project Management

FieldWire is a mobile-first construction management platform designed specifically for field teams—project managers, superintendents, and subcontractors. It's lighter than Procore but built for real field use.

## What FieldWire Does

FieldWire handles:
- Task management
- Photo documentation
- RFI workflows
- Submittals
- Daily logs
- Punch lists

## Key Features

### Mobile-First
- Built for tablets
- Offline capability
- Real-time sync
- Intuitive interface

### Task Management
- Assign tasks
- Track progress
- Due dates
- Photos attached

### Documentation
- Daily logs
- Photo capture
- Issue tracking
- Punch lists

### Collaboration
- RFI management
- Submittals
- Comments
- Notifications

## Pricing

| Plan | Price |
|------|-------|
| Essential | $500/month |
| Professional | $2,000/month |
| Enterprise | Custom |

## Pros

- Mobile-first design
- Easy to use
- Good for field teams
- Offline capability
- Affordable vs Procore

## Cons

- Less comprehensive
- Limited financials
- Smaller ecosystem
- Not for enterprise

## Verdict

FieldWire is excellent for contractors who need field-focused project management without enterprise complexity or cost.

**Best for:** Field teams, superintendents, mid-size contractors
**Skip if:** You need full financials or enterprise scale
