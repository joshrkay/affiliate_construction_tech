# Procore Review: The #1 Construction Management Platform

Procore is the gold standard for construction management software, used on over 3 million projects globally. For general contractors managing commercial and residential projects, Procore offers the most comprehensive platform in the industry.

## Use Cases

### Case 1: Commercial General Contractor
A mid-size GC uses Procore to manage 10 concurrent commercial projects. They track RFIs, submittals, and daily logs in one place, reducing coordination time by 20%.

### Case 2: Residential Developer
A residential developer uses Procore to manage subdivisions across multiple trades and phases, with real-time budget tracking.

### Case 3: Specialty Contractor
A mechanical contractor uses Procore to handle project-based work with multiple general contractors, standardized processes.

## Verdict

Procore is the gold standard for construction management. It's expensive and complex, but for serious general contractors, there's no better platform.

**Best for:** Commercial GCs, growing construction companies, companies managing multiple projects, enterprise contractors.

**Skip if:** You do simple residential projects, have a very small team, or need basic job tracking only.
