# Procore Review: The #1 Construction Management Platform

Procore is the gold standard for construction management software, used on over 3 million projects globally. For general contractors managing commercial and residential projects, Procore offers the most comprehensive platform in the industry.

## What Procore Does

Procore is an end-to-end construction management platform:
- Project management
- Field productivity
- Financial management
- Resource management
- Safety management
- Quality control
- AI-powered features

**Who it's for:** General contractors, construction managers, specialty contractors, owners and developers.

## Key Features

### Project Management
- RFI management
- Submittals
- Daily logs
- Change orders
- Punch lists
- Photo documentation

### Financial Management
- Budget tracking
- Cost reporting
- Project financials
- Contract management
- Pay application workflow

### Field Productivity
- Mobile-first design
- Daily log tools
- Task management
- Schedule integration
- BIM viewing

### Resource Management
- Crew scheduling
- Equipment tracking
- Time tracking
- Utilization reporting

### Procore AI
New AI-powered features:
- AI-powered RFIs
- Automated daily logs
- Risk identification
- Chat with your data

## Pricing

Procore pricing is tiered:

| Plan | Price | Who it's for |
|------|-------|--------------|
| Core | ~$15/user/mo | Basic PM |
| Pro | ~$50/user/mo | Most contractors |
| Enterprise | Custom | Large organizations |

Plus implementation fees. Annual billing.

## Pros

- **Industry leader** - 3M+ projects
- **Comprehensive** - every feature
- **Mobile-first** - great field tools
- **Strong integrations** - 100+ connections
- **AI features** - leading innovation
- **Scalable** - grows with you
- **Proven** - enterprise-grade

## Cons

- **Expensive** - premium pricing
- **Complex** - learning curve
- **Implementation** - takes time
- **Overkill for small GC** - too much for simple projects
- **Support varies** - can be slow

## Use Cases

### Case 1: Commercial General Contractor
A mid-size GC uses Procore to manage 10 concurrent commercial projects. They track RFIs, submittals, and daily logs in one place, reducing coordination time by 20%.

### Case 2: Residential Developer
A residential developer uses Procore to manage subdivisions across multiple trades and phases, with real-time budget tracking.

### Case 3: Specialty Contractor
A mechanical contractor uses Procore to handle project-based work with multiple general contractors, standardized processes.

## Verdict

Procore is the gold standard for construction management. It's expensive and complex, but for serious general contractors, there's no better platform.

**Best for:** Commercial GCs, growing construction companies, companies managing multiple projects, enterprise contractors.

**Skip if:** You do simple residential projects, have a very small team, or need basic job tracking only.
