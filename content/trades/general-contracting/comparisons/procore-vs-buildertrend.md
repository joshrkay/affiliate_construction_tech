# Procore vs Buildertrend: Complete Comparison

Procore and Buildertrend are two of the most popular construction management platforms—but they serve different markets. Here's the complete comparison.

## Quick Summary

| Factor | Procore | Buildertrend |
|--------|---------|--------------|
| **Price** | $15-$50/user/mo | $10-$20/user/mo |
| **Best For** | Commercial GC | Home builders |
| **Ease** | Complex | Easy |
| **Residential** | Good | Excellent |
| **Client Portal** | Limited | Strong |

## Pricing

### Procore
- Core: ~$15/user/month
- Pro: ~$50/user/month
- Enterprise: Custom
- Implementation: $5K-$15K extra

### Buildertrend
- Base: $10/user/month
- Classic: $15/user/month
- Complete: $20/user/month
- Setup fee: ~$500

**Winner:** Buildertrend for price

## Features

### Project Management
- **Procore:** Comprehensive RFIs, submittals, daily logs, punch lists
- **Buildertrend:** Strong task management, selections, change orders

### Scheduling
- **Procore:** Advanced, integration with major scheduling tools
- **Buildertrend:** Gantt charts, phase scheduling, client-facing

### Client Communication
- **Procore:** Limited
- **Buildertrend:** Excellent client portal, selections management

### Commercial Focus
- **Procore:** Enterprise-grade commercial
- **Buildertrend:** Better for residential

**Winner:** Procore for features, Buildertrend for residential ease

## Best For

### Choose Procore If:
- You do commercial work
- You need enterprise features
- You manage large projects
- You have dedicated PM staff
- 20+ employees

### Choose Buildertrend If:
- You build homes or remodel
- Client communication is key
- You want easy to use
- Budget is a concern
- Small to mid-size

## Verdict

**Procore wins for commercial general contractors** who need enterprise features.

**Buildertrend wins for home builders and remodelers** who need residential-focused tools at reasonable prices.
