# Procore vs FieldWire: Construction Software Comparison

Procore vs FieldWire is a common comparison for contractors deciding between enterprise and mobile-first construction software.

## Quick Summary

| Factor | Procore | FieldWire |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| Price | $15-$50/user | $500-$2000/mo |
| Best For | Enterprise | Field teams |
| Features | Comprehensive | Focused |
| Complexity | High | Low |

## Procore

**Pros:**
- Industry standard
- Comprehensive
- 100+ integrations
- Enterprise-grade
- Scales

**Cons:**
- Expensive
- Complex
- Implementation time

## FieldWire

**Pros:**
- Mobile-first
- Easy to use
- Offline capability
- Affordable
- Great for field

**Cons:**
- Less comprehensive
- Limited financials
- Smaller ecosystem

## Verdict

**Enterprise/commercial:** Procore
**Field-focused/small-mid:** FieldWire
