# Best Construction Accounting Software (2026)

Construction contractors need accounting software that handles job costing, progress billing, and the unique financial needs of the construction industry.

## Quick Comparison

| Software | Best For | Price |
|----------|----------|-------|
| QuickBooks | Most contractors | $30-$180/mo |
| Sage 100 Contractor | Complex needs | $200+/mo |
| Knowify | Job costing focus | $99-$599/mo |
| Foundation | Larger contractors | Custom |

## Best Overall: QuickBooks

The industry standard for small contractors. Easy to use, widely supported, integrates with everything.

**Pros:**
- Industry standard
- Easy to find help
- 100+ integrations
- Scalable

**Cons:**
- Not construction-specific
- Requires workarounds

## Best for Job Costing: Knowify

Construction-specific job costing with QuickBooks integration.

**Pros:**
- Construction-specific
- Good job costing
- Integrates with QB

**Cons:**
- Less established

## Best for Enterprise: Sage 100 Contractor

For contractors with complex accounting needs.

**Pros:**
- Construction-specific
- Strong features
- Comprehensive

**Cons:**
- Expensive
- Dated interface

## Verdict

**Most contractors:** QuickBooks
**Job costing focus:** Knowify
**Complex needs:** Sage
