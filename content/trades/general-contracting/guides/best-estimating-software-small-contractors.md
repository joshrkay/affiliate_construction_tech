## Jobber: Best Affordability

### Overview
Jobber offers an excellent balance of features and affordability, making it perfect for small construction contractors just getting started with software.

### Pros
- **Affordable**: Starts at just $49/user/month.
- **Easy to use**: Intuitive interface requires minimal training.
- **Mobile app**: Fully functional iOS/Android app for field use.
- **Automated reminders**: Reduce no-shows with text/email reminders.

### Cons
- **Limited advanced features**: Not as robust as enterprise tools.
- **Basic reporting**: Less detailed analytics than larger platforms.

### Contractor Verdict
> "Jobber gave us professional software without the enterprise price tag. Perfect for our small team." — *Mike T., General contractor, 4 employees*

---

## Buildertrend: Best All-in-One

### Overview
Buildertrend provides comprehensive project management with estimating, scheduling, and client communication—all in one platform.

### Pros
- **All-in-one platform**: Estimating, scheduling, project management, and CRM.
- **Strong client portal**: Customers can track progress, approve changes, and pay invoices.
- **Good integrations**: Connects with QuickBooks, Xero, and more.

### Cons
- **Learning curve**: More features require more setup time.
- **Pricey for very small teams**: May be more than needed for 1-5 person crews.

### Contractor Verdict
> "Buildertrend handles everything—estimating, scheduling, client communication. Worth the investment as we grew." — *Sarah K., Construction company owner, 12 employees*

## STACK Takeoff: Best Flexibility

### Overview
STACK Takeoff offers a flexible, open platform with a marketplace of plugins and extensions to customize your workflow.

### Pros
- **Plugin ecosystem**: Hundreds of extensions for specialized needs.
- **Flexible workflows**: Customizable templates and assemblies.
- **Cross-platform**: Works on any device with a browser.

### Cons
- **Setup time**: Requires more configuration upfront.
- **Plugin costs**: Some extensions add to the base price.

### Contractor Verdict
> "STACK's plugins let us build exactly what we need. The most flexible tool we've used." — *David L., General contractor, 8 employees*

## QuickBooks Enterprise: Best for Existing QB Users

### Overview
If you're already using QuickBooks for accounting, QuickBooks Enterprise offers advanced job costing and estimating built in.

### Pros
- **Seamless QuickBooks integration**: No data sync needed.
- **Advanced job costing**: Track labor, materials, and overhead accurately.
- **Robust reporting**: Detailed profitability analysis.

### Cons
- **Requires QuickBooks**: Not ideal if you're on a different platform.
- **Higher upfront cost**: Starting at $1,500/year.

### Contractor Verdict
> "Since we're already on QuickBooks, Enterprise was a natural fit. The job costing alone paid for itself." — *James P., Electrical contractor, 6 employees*

## PlanSwift: Best Desktop Power

### Overview
PlanSwift is a desktop application known for powerful measurement tools and detailed assemblies library.

### Pros
- **Powerful measurement**: Drag-and-drop, point-and-click, and scripting.
- **Large assemblies library**: Pre-built assemblies for fast estimation.
- **Detailed reports**: Extensive reporting and export options.

### Cons
- **Desktop only**: Not accessible remotely.
- **Steeper learning curve**: More complex than web-based alternatives.

### Contractor Verdict
> "PlanSwift is the gold standard for detailed takeoff. When I need precision, it's my go-to." — *Tom W., Senior estimator, 30-person company*

## How to Choose

### Consider Your Team Size
- **Very small (1-5 people)**: Jobber for affordability
- **Small-to-mid (5-15 people)**: Buildertrend for all-in-one
- **Growing (10+)**: STACK for flexibility

### Budget
- **Entry-level**: $49-100/month (Jobber, basic STACK)
- **Mid-tier**: $100-200/month (Buildertrend, full STACK)
- **Enterprise**: $200+/month (Procore, QuickBooks Enterprise)

### Key Features Needed
- **Simple estimating**: Jobber
- **Project management**: Buildertrend
- **Customization**: STACK Takeoff
- **Accounting integration**: QuickBooks Enterprise
