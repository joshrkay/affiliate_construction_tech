# How to Switch Construction Software Without Losing Data

## Planning Your Switch

Switching software is big. Here's how to do it right.

## Step 1: Export Your Data

Before leaving, export:
- Customer lists
- Job histories
- Invoice records
- Equipment lists

## Step 2: Choose New Software

Research new options:
- Read reviews
- Request demos
- Check integrations

## Step 3: Plan the Transition

Timeline:
- Week 1-2: Research and decide
- Week 3: Set up new software
- Week 4: Import data
- Week 5-6: Train team
- Week 7-8: Go live

## Step 4: Communicate with Team

Let everyone know:
- Why we're switching
- Timeline
- Training schedule

## Step 5: Go Live

Start with:
- New jobs only at first
- Keep old system for reference
- Monitor for issues

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Rushing the switch** - Take your time
2. **Not training enough** - Invest in training
3. **Not exporting data** - Always have backups
4. **Choosing cheapest** - Consider value, not just price
