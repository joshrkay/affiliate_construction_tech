# Jobber Review: Field Service Software for Painting Contractors

Jobber is a field service management platform designed for small to mid-size service businesses—including painting contractors. With pricing starting at just $29/user/month, it's one of the most accessible ways for painters to professionalize their operations.

## What Jobber Does

Jobber handles the essentials of running a painting business:
- Scheduling and dispatch
- Customer management
- Quoting and invoicing
- Payment processing
- Photo documentation
- Client communication

**Who it's for:** Small painting contractors, solo painters, growing painting businesses wanting professional tools at budget prices.

## Key Features

### Scheduling
- Visual calendar
- Technician scheduling
- Route views
- Customer reminders (SMS/email)
- Recurring job scheduling

### Client Management
- Contact database
- Property notes (colors used, square footage)
- Service history
- Client portal for online booking

### Quoting & Invoicing
- Create professional quotes
- Convert quotes to jobs
- Flat-rate and T&M options
- Online invoice acceptance
- Recurring invoicing

### Payments
- Accept credit cards, debit cards, ACH
- Automatic payment processing
- Send payment links
- Integration with QuickBooks

### Photos
- Capture before/after photos
- Add to invoices
- Build property galleries

## Pros

- **Affordable** - starts at $29/user/month
- **Easy to use** - minimal training
- **Mobile app** - full functionality in the field
- **Client portal** - online booking
- **Professional** - branded documents
- **Quick setup** - days not months
- **Good integrations** - QuickBooks, Stripe, Mailchimp

## Cons

- **Limited advanced features** - no complex job costing
- **Basic reporting** - limited analytics
- **Commercial weak** - better for residential
- **Scaling limits** - may outgrow at 20+ users
- **Marketing not included** - needs separate tools

## Use Cases

### Case 1: Solo Painter
A solo painter uses Jobber to send professional quotes and invoices. The client portal lets customers book directly, reducing phone tag.

### Case 2: Small Painting Crew
A 4-painter crew uses Jobber to schedule jobs, track time, and send invoices. They use the photo feature to show before/after for exterior paint jobs.

### Case 3: Growing Painting Business
A painting company scaling from 2 to 8 painters uses Jobber to standardize estimating with consistent markup templates.

## Verdict

Jobber is the best choice for small painting contractors who need professional tools without enterprise complexity or cost.

**Best for:** Small painting businesses (1-10 painters), solo painters, residential painters, companies new to field service software.

**Skip if:** You need advanced job costing, handle significant commercial work, or have more than 20 painters.
