# ServiceTitan Review: Field Service Software for Painting Contractors

ServiceTitan is the most comprehensive field service management platform available for painting contractors. With powerful scheduling, customer management, invoicing, and marketing tools, it's the choice of painting companies ready to scale from small operations to enterprise-level businesses.

## What ServiceTitan Does

ServiceTitan handles every aspect of running a painting contractor business:
- Smart scheduling and dispatch
- Customer relationship management
- Invoicing and payment processing
- Job tracking and documentation
- Marketing automation
- Commercial project management

**Who it's for:** Mid-to-large painting contractors, growing painting businesses, companies handling both residential and commercial work.

## Key Features

### Scheduling & Dispatch
- Visual dispatch board
- Technician routing
- Skills-based scheduling
- Customer arrival notifications
- Drag-and-drop job management

### Customer Management
- Full contact database
- Service history tracking
- Property notes (square footage, paint colors used)
- Customer portal for online booking
- Communication log

### Invoicing & Payments
- Professional invoices
- Online payment acceptance
- Recurring billing for maintenance contracts
- Automatic receipts
- Flat-rate and time-and-materials options

### Marketing
- Automated review requests
- Email campaigns
- Neighborhood campaigns
- Call tracking
- Customer lifetime value tracking

### Commercial Features
- Project-based tracking
- Change order management
- Retention tracking
- Lien waiver generation
- Multi-location support
