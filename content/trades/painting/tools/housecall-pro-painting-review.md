# Housecall Pro Review: Field Service Software for Painters

Housecall Pro is a popular all-in-one field service management platform designed for home service businesses—including painting contractors. With over 50 integrations and a focus on ease of use, it's a top choice for small to mid-size painting companies.

## What Housecall Pro Does

Housecall Pro handles:
- Smart scheduling
- Customer management
- Invoicing and payments
- Photo documentation
- Marketing tools

**Who it's for:** Small to mid-size painting contractors, painting businesses wanting professional tools without enterprise complexity.

## Key Features

### Scheduling
- Drag-and-drop calendar
- Technician routing
- Auto-scheduling
- Customer booking portal
- SMS confirmations

### Invoicing & Payments
- Professional invoices
- Online payment links
- Credit card processing
- Recurring billing

### Photo Documentation
- Before/after photos
- Add to invoices
- Property galleries
- Useful for warranty work

### Marketing
- Review requests
- Email campaigns
- Customer loyalty

## Pricing

| Plan | Price |
|------|-------|
| Essentials | $49/user/mo |
| Professional | $79/user/mo |
| Enterprise | $99/user/mo |

Most painters choose **$79/user/month**.

## Pros

- **Easy to use**
- **Affordable**
- **Mobile app**
- **Professional look**
- **Online booking**
- **Photo docs**

## Cons

- Job costing limited
- Commercial weak
- Basic reporting
- May outgrow at 20+

## Verdict

Housecall Pro is ideal for small to mid-size painting contractors wanting professional tools at reasonable prices.

**Best for:** Painting contractors with 2-15 painters.

**Skip if:** You need advanced job costing or commercial features.
