# Lead Outreach Workflow

## When We Reach Out

### During Downtime (When Not Posting)
- ✅ Review Reddit for questions
- ✅ Find contractors to engage with

---

## Lead Generation Activities

### 1. Reddit Engagement
- Search r/HVAC, r/Plumbing, r/Contractors
- Find questions about software
- Provide helpful answers with site links

### 2. Facebook Groups
- Join contractor groups
- Engage with posts
- Share relevant content

### 3. LinkedIn
- Connect with contractors
- Share industry content
- Build relationships

### 4. Direct Outreach
- Find contractor emails
- Send helpful information
- Build newsletter

---

## Questions to Ask Leads

1. **What software do you currently use?**
2. **What do you like most about it?**
3. **What do you hate about it?**
4. **What would make you switch?**
5. **What manual tasks do you wish software did?**
